Huh? Privacy Policy


April 12, 2020

Peter Yaacoub built « Huh? » as an ad-supported app.

Throughout the privacy policy, the term « Service » will refer to « Huh? » and the use of first-person singular pronouns will relate to the developer Peter Yaacoub.

This page informs visitors regarding my policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decides to use the Service.

Information Collection and Use

The Service uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify individual users.

Here is the link to the privacy policy of third party service providers used by the Service:

  • AdMob

Third-party services used for advertising may use a unique identifier provided by the user's device to present relevant and personalised ads.

Other third-party advertising services may directly collect user data to present relevant and personalised ads.

The Service does not target children under thirteen. Because of that, third-party advertising services may directly collect user data to present relevant and personalised ads.

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) can opt-out from sharing their data with AdMob and stop receiving relevant and personalised ads based on their interests.

Links to Other Sites

This Service may contain links to other sites. I do not operate these external sites. Therefore, I strongly advise users to review the Privacy Policy of these websites.

Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data, commonly used as anonymous and unique identifiers. The Service does not use these cookies explicitly. However, the Service may use third-party services or link to sites that use cookies to collect information and improve their services. The user has the option to either accept or refuse a site's cookies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, any changes will be noted in with new version releases, through the store that hosts the Service. These changes take effect immediately after this page is updated.

Contact Us
